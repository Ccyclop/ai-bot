Privacy Policy — Noda's Shoes

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Noda's Shoes ("we", "us") handles your information when you interact with our AI customer service assistant on Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and WhatsApp.

What we collect

  • Messages you send to our Facebook Page, Instagram account, or WhatsApp number
  • Your platform-provided user ID (e.g., Messenger Page-Scoped ID)
  • Your name and profile picture as provided by Meta

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiries about our products, prices, sizes, delivery, and orders
  • To improve the quality of our customer service
  • Your message text is sent to OpenAI (api.openai.com) to generate AI responses. OpenAI processes the text per their privacy policy: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not store messages longer than necessary to provide service

Data retention

Conversation history is retained for up to 30 days for service improvement, after which it is automatically deleted.

Your rights

You can request to access, correct, or delete your data at any time by contacting us at hello@nodasshoes.ge. We respond within 7 days.

Data deletion

To delete all data we have about you:

  1. Send an email to hello@nodasshoes.ge with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your Facebook/Instagram username or WhatsApp number
  3. We will delete all conversation data within 7 days and confirm by email

Contact

Questions? Email nodasshoes@gmail.com or DM us on Instagram @nodasshoes.
